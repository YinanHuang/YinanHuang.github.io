About

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Machine Learning at Georgia Tech, supervised by Prof. Pan Li. My current research mainly focuses on generative models (diffusion models and flow matching). I am also working on differential privacy and geometric deep learning (machine learning on graphs, equivariant neural networks).

Prior to that, I earned my M.S. degree in ECE at Duke University and B.S. degree in Physics at Sun Yat-sen University. I also spent one year as a research intern at Peking University, supervised by Prof. Muhan Zhang.
