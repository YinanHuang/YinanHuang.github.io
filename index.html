About

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Machine Learning at Georgia Tech, under the supervision of Prof. Pan Li. Before that, I earned my M.S. degree in ECE at Duke University and B.S. degree in Physics at Sun Yat-sen University. My research focuses on machine learning for graphs or structured data.
